Game of the year 2018
God of War is a masterful reinvention of the franchise, blending brutal action with a deep, emotional narrative. Kratos, accompanied by his son Atreus, journeys through a world inspired by Norse mythology, exploring themes of redemption, fatherhood, and legacy. The combat is visceral, with the Leviathan Axe feeling incredibly satisfying, while the graphics and art direction are breathtaking. A masterpiece that redefines what an action game can be.
One of the greatest achievements of God of War is that from the very first frame to the last, everything is presented in a single continuous shot, meaning there are no cuts anywhere, including battles and all other aspects of gameplay
- LevelUp
